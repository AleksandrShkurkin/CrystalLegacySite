About
CrystalLegacy is an ARPG with elements of survival, farming and exploration. The Protagonist, who unexpectedly got a unique ability to level up, travels the world, fights with monsters, upgarades his equipment and prepares for the final battle with a powerfull ancient beast.
Game features:
  • Dynamic fighting system – use melee and range weapons, upgrade your skills and strategically choose your allies.
  • Companions – tame slimes, goblins and other creatures that will help you in battles.
  • Farming and surival – grow crops, cook food and hold your hero in optimal form.
  • Breathtaking story – explore the world, uncover mysteries and find the truth about your power.
  • Various minigames – fishing, cooking, “dream adventures” and other fun stuff.
